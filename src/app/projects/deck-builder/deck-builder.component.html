Deck Builder

A tool that allows you to open packs of cards for Hearthstone and draft a deck.

Features

  • Used for official Redbull draft tournaments
  • Modelled after Hearthstone in-game pack opener
  • Uses HTML Audio
  • Share your draft in Facebook and Twitter

Technologies Used

  • Angular
  • Node.js
  • MongoDB
  • AWS
  • HTML5 + CSS3
